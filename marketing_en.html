Why CapFinder?

CapFinder is an app created for the keyboard community. Finding the keycap you want among countless options can be difficult. CapFinder helps you search for keycaps in various ways, including by color, name, and designer.

How to Use

  1. When you launch the app, you'll see a search box and color filter on the home screen.
  2. Enter the keycap name or designer name in the search box.
  3. Or select your desired color from the color filter.
  4. Select the keycap you want from the search results to view detailed information.
  5. Use the sort options to sort by release date or name.
